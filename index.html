Sheet-to-Social Media Publisher

The Problem

A content team was spending hours manually writing platform-specific posts, generating images, and copy-pasting to LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook. The process was slow, inconsistent, and bottlenecked on one person.

Outcome: One row added to Google Sheets triggers a fully automated pipeline — Perplexity AI researches context, GPT-4 writes platform-tailored copy, DALL-E generates images, and posts publish simultaneously to all four platforms. Hands-free.

QuickRun Errand Operations Automation

The Problem

An errand service was managing customer orders manually — logging requests by hand, sending status updates individually, and losing track of which orders were assigned, in-progress, or completed. Customer experience suffered and staff spent more time on admin than on delivery.

Outcome: A dual-flow n8n system now handles the full order lifecycle. New submissions are automatically logged to a CRM sheet and trigger a branded confirmation email. Status changes fire instant customer updates — Assigned, In Progress, Out for Delivery, Completed — with zero manual sending.
